Welcome to the Mangonada Fan Club! Here you can find all the latest news and updates about the best drink in the world, the Mangonada.

The Mangonada is a drink that is made with mangos, sugar, lime juice, and water.

The Mangonada originated in Mexico and is a popular drink in the country.

Ingredients

  • Mangos
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice

Recipe

  1. Cut the mangos into small pieces.
  2. Add the mangos to a blender.
  3. Add the sugar and lime juice to the blender.
  4. Blend the ingredients until smooth.
